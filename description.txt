A mod that simply removes some of the nerfs Calamity does to the vanilla game.

Available un-nerfs (each can by disabled or enabled in config):

Un-Nerfs
- Soaring Insignia Flight: Grants infinite flight and rocket boots again (default:enabled)
- Soaring Insignia Movement: Grants orginal jump heigth and acceleration (default:disabled)
- Magiluminescence: Grants original acceleration (default:enabled)
- Rod Of Discord: Allow the rod of discord to be used while chaos state is active (default:disabled)
- Magic Mirror: Allow use of magic mirror/recall potion while fighting a boss (default:enabled)
- Teleporters: Allow use of teleporters while fighting a boss (default:disabled)
- Yoyo Bag And Glove: Remove damage nerf to yoyos (default:enabled)

Damage/Defense/Endurance Un-Nerfs
- Defense Damage: Disable defense damage (default:disabled)
- Summon Damage: Disable nerf your summon damage while holding another class weapon (default:disabled)
- Damage Reduction Cap: Returns the vanilla damage reduction, with no limitations (default:disabled)
- Worm Scarf: Grants vanilla 17% DR (default:disabled)
- Endurance Potion: Grants vanilla 10% DR (default:disabled)
- Frozen Turtle Shell: Grants vanilla 25% (default:disabled)

Crafts
- Zenith Vanilla Craft: Adds vanilla recipe for zenith (default:disabled)


Any change in configuration requires reload

If you have any requests let me know!

NOTE: The tooltips will continue to show the calamity information but the values in the background are the un-nerfed ones, I'm just too lazy to modify each tooltip
